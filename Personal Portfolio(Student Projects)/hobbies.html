Hobbies & Interests

I enjoy illustration, pastel color studies, and journaling. These hobbies influence the aesthetic choices in my portfolio — I choose soft palettes and rounded shapes to create a calm, friendly experience. When I'm learning new UI patterns, I sketch quick mockups on paper or digital tools before coding them. I find that practicing visual composition and color harmony improves my ability to make cleaner interfaces. I also occasionally cook or bake simple recipes, which I photograph for the gallery and sometimes include as blog posts. These activities help me stay creative and balanced while managing coursework.
